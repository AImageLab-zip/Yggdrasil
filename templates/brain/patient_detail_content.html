Brain MRI Viewer Instructions
Multi-Window Grid Viewer
Loading Modalities
  • Drag and drop: Drag a modality chip onto any viewer window (it replaces the current modality automatically)
  • Multiple views: Load up to 4 different modalities simultaneously
  • Comparison: View T1, T2, FLAIR, and T1c side-by-side
Navigation Controls
  • Scroll wheel: Navigate through slices
  • A/S/C buttons: Switch between Axial, Sagittal, and Coronal views
  • Left-click + drag: Adjust window/level (brightness/contrast)
  • Ruler tool: Click the ruler above the grid, then click two points (can be on different slices/views). Hover a point or line to see the 3D Euclidean distance in mm.
  • Free Scroll button (link icon): Toggle synchronization — By default, windows with the same orientation scroll together. Click to unlink and scroll independently, or click again to re-sync.
Window Management
  • Right-click: Clear window (remove modality)
  • Re-drag: Replace modality in any window
